    This Section provides a framework for understanding the gretaer economic role of renewable energy through its impact on Countries’ GDP

  • Energy fuels global economic activity. As populations expand, living standards improve and consumption rises, total demands for energy is expected to increase by 21% by 2030((The International Renewable Energy Agency-IRENA)) 2015)
  • Among other factors, the magnitude of the impacts of renewable energy on GDP will depend on the economic structure of the country
  • Doubling the share of renewables in the global GDP in 2030 by up to 1.1% equivalent to USA 1.3. trillion
  • Doubling the share of renewables by 2030 has a positive impact on global welfare, which increases by 2.7% compared to a 0.6% GDP improvement.
