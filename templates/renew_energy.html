    What happened last 10 years?

  • Globally, around one-quarter of our electircity comes from renewables
  • Since the Industrial Revolution, the energy mix of most countries across the world has become dominated by fossil fuels. This has major implications for the global climate, as well as for human health. Fossil fuels are responsible for large amounts of local air pollution- a health problem that leads to at least 5 million premature deaths each year.
  • To reduce CO2 emissions and local air pollution, Renewable energy will play a key role in the decabonization of our energy systems in the coming decades.
  • Overall Renewable Energy in 2010 20.21%, in 2019 33%
  • In 10 years, Solar Enery production 0.13% in 2010 to 1.76% in 2019
  • In 10 years, Wind Energy production 1.017% in 2010 to 3.36% in 2019
  • In 2019, around 16% of global electricity came from hydropower
  • In 2019, around 5% of global electricity came from wind
  • In 2019, around 2% of global electricity came from solar
